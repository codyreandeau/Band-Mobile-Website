About us:

'Morning People' is a space-folk band who currently reside in Albany, NY. They started out as a three-piece band in Tupper Lake, NY, composed of Mark Pratico on Vocals/Guitar/Keyboard, Emily Mitchell on Vocals/Guitar/Keyboards and Cody Reandeau on Drums. After the relocation to Albany, the band decided they wanted to expand their band, and become a four-piece. Soon, Keith Machabee was added to the band as their new drummer, and Cody moved to bass. The current line-up is:
  • Emily Mitchell-Vocals, Guitar, Keyboards
  • Mark Pratico-Vocals, Guitar, Keyboards
  • Cody Reandeau-Bass
  • Keith Machabee-Drums
